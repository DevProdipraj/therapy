About the Principal
Psychologist & Hypnotherapist

Samantha Tay

She is a Principal Clinical Psychologist & Hypnotherapist, experience in administering psychological assessment, integrated psychotherapy & Hypnotherapy. Using different psychotherapeutic modalities such as CBT, EMDR, REBT and Hypnotherapy for the treatment of Depression, PTSD and other psychological disorders. She is also a member of the Association ofsychotherapist and Counsellors (APAC) in Singapore.She has mastered he art of Hypnotherapy and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy which she employs to treat psychological issues effectively.Her techniques have shown promising outcomes for her clients to battle against issues like Panic Attack, Anxiety, Youth & Adolescent issues, Grief and other Trauma & mental health issues.She does not only help people overcome their psychological issues but also helps them unleash their hidden talents and discover their ultimate potential.Her special interests include corporate coaching, crisis management and career counselling.

Our Philosophy

Highs and lows are a part of life, and life must go on. To keep our lives going on, we need certain conflicts and issues unaddressed. These issues remain under the surface until they all pile up and become a mountain that then starts trying to rupture our emotional skin and break out.At such moments, people usually get puzzled and can't make sense of what is happening to them as they lose track of their past issues. It does not represent any emotional weakness or mental instability. It is natural and happens everybody.Sometimes, talking with your close friends and family members helps suppress the issues and give you a break until they break out again. And sometimes, it is just not enough.That is when you need professional help like the one we offer. It would help if you had an environment where you can talk to somebody who listens, understands and can propose solutions in a non- judgmental and private manner.
